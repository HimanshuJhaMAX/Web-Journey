Omlette

Today, We are going to learn the quick recipe for making an omlette

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Frying Pan
  • Butter or Oil
  • Salt
  • Kitchen appliances

Instructions

  1. Turn on the gas stove to heat in mid-flame.
  2. Take a bowl and add eggs.
  3. Add salt accourding to taste.
  4. Fix it well until the pan gets heated.
  5. After the pan gets heated, lower the flame and add some butter or oil.
  6. Pour the egg in the pan and cook until it is about to get done.
  7. Enjoy your tasty Egg.
